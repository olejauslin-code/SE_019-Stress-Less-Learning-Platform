About Us

Stress-Less is a tool for you and your family to learn and understand online safety. We will teach you everything there is to know about how social media affects your brain and how to use it safely and productively.

Who are We?

Stress-Less was founded in Berlin by students at CODE University. Our team consists of mostly students in Berlin, but we regularly work with experts in pedagogy to ensure that our platforms are child-friendly and that the information we teach is accurate.

Our Goals

Social media is often related to negative mental health effects and online safety issues. We think that while these are real issues, social media is a valuable tool both for socializing and realizing your ambitions. We want to help parents teach the next generation how to get all of the benefits social media has to offer while negating the negative effects it can have on their health.
